# 🌱 Mint Rewards Brand Hub - Dashboard Overview

## Executive Summary

The **Mint Rewards Brand Hub** is a comprehensive sustainability analytics and brand management platform designed for companies participating in environmental waste management and recycling programs. This dashboard provides deep insights into environmental impact, user engagement, and business performance while maintaining complete data privacy and confidentiality.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features Overview

### 🎯 **Core Analytics Tabs**

The dashboard is organized into **6 main analytical sections**, each providing specific insights for brand managers:

## 1. 📈 **Impact Analytics** 
*Screenshots: Company Impact & User Impact Analysis*

### **Company Impact Tracking**
![Company Impact Dashboard](./screenshots/01%20-%20Dashboard%20-%20Company%20Impact.png)

- **Waste Collection Monitoring**: Track exactly how much waste your company collects by material type
- **CO₂ Savings Calculation**: Real-time environmental impact measurement using scientific conversion rates
- **Material Breakdown**: Detailed analysis of Paper, Cardboard, Plastic, Glass, Aluminum, and Electronic waste
- **Environmental Metrics**: Precise tracking of your company's direct sustainability contributions

### **User Community Impact**
![User Impact Dashboard](./screenshots/02%20-%20Dashboard%20-%20Users%20Impact.png)

- **Estimated User Collections**: Aggregated impact from your user base
- **Environmental Equivalents**: Translate CO₂ savings into understandable metrics:
  - Trees planted equivalent
  - Driving distance saved  
  - LED bulb hours powered
- **Community Statistics**: Total user contributions without revealing individual identities

**Key Benefits:**
- ✅ Track both corporate and community environmental impact
- ✅ Scientific CO₂ calculations for accurate reporting
- ✅ Visual charts showing waste distribution and trends
- ✅ Privacy-focused user analytics
